FAQs

What time is it?

We recommend arriving between 12-12:30pm. The ceremony will take place at 1pm. There will be food at about 3pm, and partying including Jazz Beams DJs until 11pm.

What's the dress code?

Anything you like! Seriously. But most people are up for dressing a bit posh at any wedding, so that's your safest bet for fitting in.

What's for tea?

There will be a large spread of buffet food, kindly prepared by mad-keen and long-suffering family members. This should satisfy all wonky dietary requirements. We will also provide some bubbly, wine, beer and soft drinks.

What shall I bring?

A bottle! Any beverage contribution, however small, would help. We suspect your appetites will be far deeper than our pockets.

Am I supposed to give you guys a gift?

Nope, definitely not. But we'd encourage donations to Strange Cargo - you already knew you loved and valued SC, and now more than ever! You'll be able to give a little cash on the day.

Can I bring the kids?

Heck yes you can. Unless you were looking forward to having a break from them for the day!
